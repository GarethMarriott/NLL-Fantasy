About Future Picks

📌 Dynamic Order

Pick order automatically rotates each year based on league standings. Worst teams draft first for competitive balance.

🔄 Trading

Teams can trade future picks along with players. Original owner is tracked to maintain draft seeding consistency.

📊 Pick Tracking

Each pick shows round number, pick number, current owner, and original owner. View all picks in the team detail page.
