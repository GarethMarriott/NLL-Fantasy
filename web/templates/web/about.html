Welcome to NLL Fantasy

Your go-to destination for managing and enjoying fantasy lacrosse. Whether you're a seasoned fantasy player or just getting started, NLL Fantasy gives you the tools you need to compete and have fun. Our goal is to grow this site into a full-featured fantasy lacrosse platform over time—helping fans get more involved, grow the game of lacrosse, and bring new fans to the NLL.

Rosters

Each fantasy team may carry a maximum of 12 players:

  • 11 Runners
    • Up to 6 Forwards
    • Up to 6 Defence
  • 1 Goalie

Best Ball Scoring Format

NLL Fantasy uses a Best Ball scoring format. Each week, only the highest-scoring players at each position contribute to your team's total score:

  • Top 3 Forwards
  • Top 3 Defence
  • Top 1 Goalie

Transition Players: Listed as Transition may count as either a Forward or Defence, depending on which position slot you have assigned them on your roster for that week.

League Type Explained

🔄 Redraft Leagues

Every season, all teams return to the draft and start fresh. Players are not retained from the previous season. This means each team has an equal opportunity to build their roster from scratch. Perfect for those who enjoy the excitement of the annual draft and want a level playing field each year.

👑 Dynasty Leagues

Teams keep their players year to year, building a legacy roster. Each offseason, you can make trades, pick up rookies, and plan your dynasty. This creates more strategic depth as you manage your team over multiple seasons. Teams that manage well maintain competitive advantages, while newer teams must build through the draft and trades.

Scoring Format Comparison

⚡ Best Ball

Set it and forget it! You draft your team and submit your roster at the start of the week. Your score is then automatically calculated using only your highest-scoring players at each position.

Key benefit: No need to manage weekly lineups. Your best players always count toward your score automatically.

🎮 Traditional

You actively choose your starting lineup each week. Only the players you select as starters contribute to your score. This requires more active management but rewards strategy and weekly decision-making.

Key benefit: Complete control over your lineup. Maximize your score by strategically choosing who plays each week.
