About

Welcome to NLL Fantasy, your go-to destination for managing and enjoying fantasy lacrosse.
Our platform lets you view team rosters, track player statistics, follow schedules, and check
league standings—all in one place. Whether you’re a seasoned fantasy player or just getting
started, NLL Fantasy gives you the tools you need to compete and have fun. Good luck, and
enjoy the season! Our goal is to grow this site into a full-featured fantasy lacrosse platform over time—helping
fans get more involved, grow the game of lacrosse, and bring new fans to the NLL.

Rosters

Each fantasy team may carry a maximum of 12 players: * Editable by Commissioner
11 Runners
Up to 6 Forwards
Up to 6 Defence
2 Goalies

Scoring

NLL Fantasy uses a Best Ball scoring format. Each week, only the highest-scoring
players at each position contribute to your team’s total score: Top 3 Forwards
Top 3 Defence
Top 1 Goalie
Players listed as Transition may count as either a Forward or Defence, depending on
which position slot you have assigned them on your roster for that week.
