About

Welcome to the NLL Fantasy website , your go-to platform for managing your fantasy lacrosse teams. Here, you can view team rosters,
player statistics, schedules, and league standings. Whether you're a seasoned fantasy player or
just getting started, our site provides all the tools you need to enjoy the NLL Fantasy experience.
Good luck and have fun!
We are hoping this site will grow into a full-featured fantasy lacrosse platform over time. helping
fans get involved and grow the game of lacrosee and gain more fans.

Rosters will have a Maximum of:
12 total Players
11 total Runners
6 Forwards
6 Defence
2 Goalies

Scoring will be a Best Ball format, where only the highest scoring players at each position contribute
to the team's total score. It will take the top 3 Forwards, top 3 Defence, and top 1 Goalie from the roster each week to calculate
the team's score. Players listed as transition will count as either a Forward or Defence, depending on
which yields the highest score for the team that week.
